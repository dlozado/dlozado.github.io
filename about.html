About Me

I am a 10th-grade student at The Browning School, where I have been since 9th grade. I grew up and currently live in Queens, New York City.

I am fully Ecuadorian, though I was born in New York City. I am the son of immigrant parents who came to New York City around 25 years ago. I also have an older brother who is currently in college.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing video games, designing clothes, and playing sports such as baseball and soccer.

In the future, I would like to work for a major finance firm. However, my ultimate dream is to own a large and successful clothing brand.
